Introducing ImaraMail.

Our own high capacity mail system developed to meet modern email needs and protect against the ever-growing email threats. ImaraMail-1.0 is already in use in our cloud production environment and we are able to set up, manage and support your very own instance to host in your office setup or as a personal Virtual Private Server in the cloud.

ImaraMail (version 1.0) includes:

  1. Standard modules
    • A high performance UNIX based Operating System.
    • Very High security module.
    • WebGUI.
    • Large File attachment support *(contact for more info).
    • AntiSpam and AntiVirus system.
    • Secure Webmail Clients.
    • Personal Vacation/Out Office setups.
    • Works with Outlook, Thunderbird or other IMAP/POP3 clients.
    • Inbuilt Dynamic Firewall – able to monitor activity and react to emerging threats.
  2. Optional modules
    • Inbuilt Disaster Recovery Module – Full replication and backup.
    • Inbuilt Backup and FileSharing system.

Our ImaraMail email system is based on Open Source software which includes:

  • Customized and Hardened FreeBSD operating system.
  • Hardened Postfix.

ImaraMail Security Firewall.

Ideal for ISPs, Email service providers or to protect any email system, our ImaraMail Security Firewall can protect your email service. Available as a cloud service or as a physical solution.

  • Monitoring Module.

ImaraMail Backup.

Our backup solution, ImaraMail Backup, can support your business and allow users to store the contacts and files on the cloud or share their files over a local network (in the office) or over the Internet. The ImaraMail Backup server is available as cloud service or physical server.
